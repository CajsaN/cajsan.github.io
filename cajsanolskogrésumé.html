Résumé Cajsa Nolskog

I’m Cajsa Nolskog, a swede living in Salem, Oregon, who loves to learn, experience and tell stories.

I’m currently studying to be a producer, and tell stories either through filmmaking, exhibition or audio productions. I would describe myself as pretty independent, and good at adjusting to new situations and people. I have an interest for the world around me, and have since long ago grown a passion for getting to know new places, cultures and their people on a deeper level. For me it’s about challenging myself in new situations, learn from other perspectives, and to get a deeper understanding for the world in general, and then hopefully share those understandings.

I enjoy to work hard on projects I believe in, and to challenge limits. I have experienced that I usually can achieve more than I believed I could. I have during my work experiencesw and studies learned to solve problems, and to produce and make things I didn’t think possible. Within us there is so much unknown potential, which is something I love to learn over and over again. Contact me for projects with audio, video or exhibition, or other work with contact to the public.
