Flying doormats since 1423

In 1423 Agata Blinn sold her first flying doormat at a market in London, and since then Flyme Doormat has evolved to be the world top provider of flying doormats. Agata taught her daughter and son the ancient spells she said were known within her family, and to this day ancestors of Agata Blinn run the company from the headquaters in Portland, Oregon. During the years, the Flyme Doormat has been perfected and today our flying doormats are faster and safer than ever. But the base spell in our Flyme Doormat has not changed since Agata first made the family secret available to the world, and we are proud to provide the worlds best flying doormats since 1423.


There are currently 2 764 046 active Flyme Doormats around the world that anyone with a Flyme Doormat can use as their destination. Many companies, families and institutions around the world have already installed their Flyme Doormat and the numbers are ever growing!
