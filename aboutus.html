Flyme Doormat Team

Sharon Blinn, Chief Executive Officer
sharon@flymedoormat.com

"Confucious once said: 'Choose a job you love, and you will never have to work a day in your life'. That sums up my life here at Flyme Doormat, I love my job!"

Flyme Doormat CEO Sharon Blinn has worked for Flyme Doormat since 1977, and took over her current position in 2003. She uses her Flyme Doormat every day to get to the office, but not before visiting her mother who live in Phoenix, AZ.


Meryem Blinn, Chief Strategy Officer
meryem@flymedoormat.com

"Individual commitment to a group effort - that is what makes a team work, a company work, a society work, a civilization work."

Flyme Doormat Chief Information Officer Meryem Blinn started her career at Flyme Doormat when she was only 14 years old as a lab assistant in the department of spell development. Meryem has been committed to Flyme Doormat since, and has worked in all from market analysis to customer service. She has been in her current position since 2014.


George Blinn, Chief Information Officer
