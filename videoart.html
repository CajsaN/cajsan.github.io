Two Chairs

Project 5 – Installation

Documentation of the video art piece Two Chairs, showed at Willamette University Student Art Exhibition - Videodrama, 2015. Projection on chairs and wall.

In this piece you are in the present moment; the room with Two Chairs. But memories and fantasies of an other, nameless, time also flash by. What can ideas, memory and fantasy make to plain, everyday place?
